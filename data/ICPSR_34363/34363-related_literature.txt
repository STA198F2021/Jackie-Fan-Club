Bibliography
Social Justice Sexuality Project: 2010 National Survey, including Puerto
Rico

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are related 
to ICPSR study 34363. It includes works that are based on primary
or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique those
data or the collection methodology. 

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR study used to:
icpsr-biblio-notify@umich.edu

based on sexual orientation." Behavioral Sciences. Feb 2019, 9, (2), 16.
DOI: 10.3390/bs9020016

2. Keene, Lance C.; Heath, Ryan D.; Bouris, Alida, "Disclosure of sexual
identities across social-relational contexts: Findings from a national
sample of black sexual minority men." Journal of Racial and Ethnic Health
Disparities. In press 2021,. DOI: 10.1007/s40615-020-00944-y

3. Kim, HaeDong; Allen, Samuel H., "A double-edged sword: Dual-identity

Journal of Homosexuality. in press 2021,. DOI:
10.1080/00918369.2021.1945339

4. Lawson-Williams, Donnalee M., "The Relationship Between Hispanics/Latino
Men Who Have Sex with Men and Women: Cultural Beliefs, Risk Behaviors and
Self-Disclosure." Dissertation, Walden University, 2017.

5. Oyarvide-Tuthill, Zelma; Gorman, Bridget; Kumar, Navya, "Union status
and overweight or obesity among sexual minority men and women." In
Wilkinson, Lindsey; Reczek, Corinne; Liu, Hui, Marriage and Health: The

Issues in Local Contexts. 102 - 115, New Brunswick, NJ: Rutgers University
Press,2020.

6. Robinson, Brittany L., "Predictors of Sexual Orientation Disclosure in a
